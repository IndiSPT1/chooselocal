Bamboo Baskets

Materials Needed:

  • Fresh bamboo (preferably young and flexible)
  • Sharp knife or cutter
  • Water (for soaking bamboo)
  • Sandpaper (optional, for smoothing)
  • Mold or base form (optional but helpful)

Steps to Make Bamboo Baskets:

  1. Prepare the bamboo:

Cut bamboo into thin, even strips. Soak the strips in water overnight to make them more flexible. Smooth the strips using sandpaper if needed.

  1. Make the Base:

Lay out several strips in a crisscross pattern. Weave horizontal strips over and under the vertical ones to form the bottom. Make sure the base is tight.

  1. Weave the Sides:

Bend the ends of vertical strips upward. Start weaving horizontal strips around them. Alternate the pattern and press tightly after each row.

  1. Finish the Top:

Trim excess vertical strips and fold/tuck them for a smooth edge. Use a thicker strip for a handle, securing it by weaving or tying.

  1. Final Touches:

Let the basket dry completely. You can varnish or polish it for a shiny finish.
