Bamboo Penholder

Materials Needed

  • A thick bamboo stalk (around 7–10 cm in diameter)
  • Hand saw or small cutter
  • Sandpaper
  • Varnish or paint (optional)
  • Decorations like thread, cloth, or stickers (optional)

Choose and Cut Bamboo

Pick a bamboo stalk with one node (divider) at the bottom, this acts as the base. Cut a piece about 10–15cm tall, like a small cup.

Smooth the Edges

Use sandpaper to smooth the top and bottom edges.
Make sure there are no sharp splinters.

Decorate (Optional)

You can:
  • Wrap colored thread or cloth around the middle.
  • Paint patterns, your name, or traditional designs.
  • Add beads or stickers.
  • Varnish for a shiny, polished finish.
