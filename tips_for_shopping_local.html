Tips For Shopping Local










  • Explore Local Markets (Visit morning or weekend markets where local vendors gather)
  • Talk to the Sellers (Who made it, how it’s made, and what it’s made from. You might learn interesting stories and build connections with the makers)
  • Carry Cash (Many small vendors don’t accept cards or digital payments)
  • Compare Before You Buy (Look at prices and quality at a few stalls before deciding)
  • Buy Handmade or Homegrown (These are often more unique and eco-friendly)
  • Choose Reusable or Natural Items (Helps the environment and supports sustainable practices)
  • Be Respectful When Bargaining (Every purchase helps the seller and their family)
  • Bring Your Own Bag (Reduce plastic use by carrying a tote bag or basket)
